About Bill Barker


The system is still under initial development. User accounts are not yet available, and neither is the search functionality. Initial viewing and layout is currently under going testing for articles.


We here at Bill Barker are committed to bring you the most up to date legislation across the United States of America. As well as the current news circulating the media regarding your local and national laws, bills, and political policies.



The Bill Barker application is an RSS Feed for Bills and Articles related to the legislation within the United States of America.

Integrated with multiple sources, the system is intended to allow users to view, search, and comment on different Articles and Bills.

Consumers who create an account will gain the commenting ability, and will also be able to make customized feed systems pertaining to specific topics, areas, and legislation.
